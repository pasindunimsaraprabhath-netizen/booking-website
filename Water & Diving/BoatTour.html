Boat Tour

Experience the beauty of Sri Lanka from a unique perspective with an unforgettable boat tour.

Best Location

1. Mirissa

Popular for ocean cruises, dolphin spotting, and coastal sightseeing.

2. Trincomalee

Provides coastal boat tours with stunning ocean views and opportunities to spot marine life.

3. Negombo Lagoon

Offers beautiful lagoon tours through fishing villages and mangrove ecosystems.

4. Madu River

A famous river safari destination featuring mangrove forests, small islands, and diverse birdlife.

5. Benthota

Known for scenic river cruises, water sports, and relaxing boat rides.

pecies You May Encounter

1. Water Monitor Lizards
2. Crocodiles (in selected river systems)
3. Kingfishers
4. Herons
5. Egrets
6. Cormorants

Quick Facts

  • Mirissa , Trincomalee, Negombo Lagoon, Madu River, Benthota
  • Early morning for calmer seas and better visibility
