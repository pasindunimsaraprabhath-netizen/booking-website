Whale Snorkeling

Whale Snorkeling is one of Sri Lanka's most unique marine experiences, allowing travelers to enter the ocean and observe majestic whales in their natural habitat.

Best Location

1. Mirissa

The most popular whale-watching destination in Sri Lanka. Deep ocean waters lie close to shore, creating ideal conditions for spotting blue whales and sperm whales. Whale season generally runs from November to April.

2. Trincomalee

Known for calmer seas and seasonal whale activity between March and July. Sperm whales, blue whales, and pilot whales are commonly reported in the area.

pecies You May Encounter

1. Blue Whale
2. Sperm Whale
3. Pilot Whale
4. Bryde's Whale
5. Spinner Dolphins
6. Bottlenose Dolphins

Quick Facts

  • Mirissa , Trincomalee
  • Early morning for calmer seas and better visibility
