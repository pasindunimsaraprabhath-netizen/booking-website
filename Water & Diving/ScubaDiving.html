Scuba Diving

Discover a vibrant underwater world filled with colorful coral reefs, tropical fish, historic shipwrecks, sea turtles, rays, and diverse marine life.

Best Location

1. Mirissa

Offers reef dives and seasonal opportunities to observe larger marine species.

2. Trincomalee

Features crystal-clear waters, coral gardens, and excellent visibility during the east coast diving season.

3. Hikkaduwa

Known for shallow coral reefs, abundant marine life, and beginner-friendly dive sites.

4. Unawatuna

Famous for reef diving, wreck dives, and encounters with sea turtles.

5. Kalpitiya

Popular for marine biodiversity, reef systems, and seasonal dolphin sightings.

Marine Life You May Encounter

1. Sea Turtles
2. Reef Sharks
3. Stingrays
4. Moray Eels
5. Lionfish
6. Parrotfish
7. Butterflyfish
8. Angelfish
9. Barracuda
10. Octopus
11. Cuttlefish
12. Colorful Coral Reefs

Quick Facts

  • Mirissa , Trincomalee, Hikkaduwa, Unawatuna, Kalpitiya
  • Early morning for calmer seas and better visibility
