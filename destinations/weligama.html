Surf & Sun

Weligama Beach

A vibrant surf town on the southern coast known for beginner-friendly waves and iconic stilt fishermen.

Catch the Perfect Wave

Weligama, translating to 'Sandy Village,' is an eclectic mix of traditional fishing village and modern surfer paradise. Located on Sri Lanka's beautiful southern coast, its sprawling, two-kilometer-long bay offers a gentle, sandy break that is perfect for those looking to learn how to surf.

Beyond the surf schools and beachfront cafes, the bay is famous for Taprobane Island, a tiny, picturesque rock island topped with a sprawling villa built in the 1920s. Just offshore, you'll also spot the iconic stilt fishermen perching gracefully above the water at dawn and dusk.

Whether you want to ride the waves, indulge in fresh seafood, or simply relax on the golden sands, Weligama has a laid-back charm that captures the essence of a tropical getaway.
