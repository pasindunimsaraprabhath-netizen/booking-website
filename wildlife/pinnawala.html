Pinnawala Elephant Orphanage

Pinnawala Elephant Orphanage is one of Sri Lanka’s most famous wildlife attractions and the largest captive elephant care facility in the country..

History & Majesty

Founded by Sri Lanka’s Department of Wildlife Conservation in 1975, the orphanage initially housed a small number of orphaned elephant calves. It was later transferred to the Department of National Zoological Gardens and expanded into a major conservation and breeding center. Over the years, dozens of elephant calves have been born at the facility, making it one of the world's most successful captive elephant breeding programs.

Pinnawala Elephant Orphanage offers a unique opportunity to observe Asian elephants up close while learning about their care and conservation. The daily river bathing sessions, large elephant herd, and educational experiences make it one of Sri Lanka’s most popular family-friendly attractions..
